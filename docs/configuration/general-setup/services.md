# Services

**Services** are additional offerings your property provides to guests — such as airport transfers, laundry, spa, or room service. Configuring services allows them to be selected and tracked in reservations and housekeeping.

## Access

**Configuration → General Configuration → Services**.

## Services List

The list shows all configured services with their name, description, and active status.

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Name | Service name (e.g. Airport Transfer, Laundry, Spa). |
| Description | Optional description of the service. |
| Active | Whether the service is currently available for selection. |

## Adding a Service

1. Click **Add** or **New Service**.
2. Fill in:
   - **Name** — required. Short, clear name.
   - **Description** — optional. Details about the service.
   - **Active** — check to make it available.
3. Save.

## Editing a Service

Click a service row to open it. Edit the fields and save.

## Deactivating a Service

Uncheck **Active** to hide the service from selection without deleting it. Existing records linked to the service are not affected.

## Common FAQs

**Where are services used in the PMS?**
Services can be linked to reservations (as additional services requested by the guest) and to housekeeping tasks or track-it items depending on your configuration.

**Can I delete a service?**
Deleting a service that is linked to existing records may cause errors. Deactivate it instead by unchecking Active.
