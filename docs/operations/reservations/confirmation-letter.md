# Reservation Confirmation Letter

The **Reservation Confirmation Letter** is a printable document sent to the guest to confirm their booking. It shows the reservation details, stay dates, room type, rate, and property contact information.

:::tip **Having a problem?**
See [Reservations Overview](/docs/operations/reservations/overview) for common FAQs and troubleshooting.
:::

## Access

From a reservation detail page, click **Confirmation Letter** (or the confirmation letter icon/button in the reservation actions).

## What It Contains

The confirmation letter typically includes:

| Section | Content |
|---------|---------|
| Property Header | Hotel name, address, phone, email, logo. |
| Guest Details | Guest name, email, phone. |
| Reservation Details | Reservation number, arrival date, departure date, number of nights. |
| Room Details | Room type, room number (if assigned), number of guests. |
| Rate | Rate plan name and nightly rate. |
| Special Requests | Any special requests noted on the reservation. |
| Booking Info | Reservation type, source, market code. |
| Footer | Property contact details and any terms/conditions. |

## Generating the Letter

1. Open the reservation (from the reservation list or detail page).
2. Click **Confirmation Letter**.
3. A modal opens with a formatted preview of the letter.
4. Click **Print** to print, or save as PDF.

The letter can be printed and handed to the guest, or saved as PDF and emailed.

## When to Use

- At the time of booking — send to the guest as proof of reservation.
- At check-in — provide a copy if the guest did not receive one earlier.
- For corporate bookings — send to the company contact for their records.

## Common FAQs

**The confirmation letter shows no rate.**
The reservation may not have a rate assigned. Ensure a rate plan and room type are selected on the reservation.

**The hotel logo or address is wrong.**
Hotel information is configured in **Configuration → General Configuration → Hotel Information**. Ask your administrator to update it.

**Can I email the confirmation letter directly from the PMS?**
The current version generates a printable/PDF version. Email it manually by saving as PDF and attaching to an email.
