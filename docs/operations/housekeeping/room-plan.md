# Housekeeping Room Plan

The **Housekeeping Room Plan** is a calendar-style grid showing room availability and occupancy over a date range. It is used to visualise which rooms are occupied, vacant, or blocked, and to create new reservations directly from the grid.

:::tip **Having a problem?**
See [Housekeeping Overview](/docs/operations/housekeeping/overview) for common FAQs and troubleshooting.
:::

## Access

From the sidebar: **Housekeeping** → **Room Plan**.

There is also a **Front Desk → Room Plan** view — both show the same room plan grid. The housekeeping room plan is accessed from the housekeeping section of the sidebar.

## Layout

The room plan is a grid where:
- **Rows** = individual rooms (grouped by room type or floor).
- **Columns** = dates across the selected date range.
- **Cells** = the status of that room on that date.

### Cell Colours

| Colour | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Green / Occupied | Room has an in-house or confirmed reservation on that date. |
| Grey / Vacant | Room is vacant and available. |
| Red / Out of Order | Room is marked out of order for that date. |
| Yellow / Dirty | Room is vacant but not yet cleaned. |

Hover over a cell to see the reservation details (guest name, reservation number, arrival/departure).

## Date Range

Use the **Start Date** and **End Date** controls to set the date range displayed. The grid scrolls horizontally across the selected range.

The default view typically shows 14–30 days from the current business date.

## Creating a Reservation from the Room Plan

Click on a **vacant cell** for a room and date to start a new reservation:

1. Click the vacant cell for the desired room and start date.
2. The new reservation form opens pre-filled with that room and date.
3. Complete the reservation form (guest, dates, rate, etc.).
4. Save. The reservation appears on the grid.

This is the fastest way to create a walk-in or same-day reservation when you can see room availability at a glance.

## Viewing a Reservation

Click on an **occupied cell** (a reservation bar) to open the reservation detail or a quick-view popup showing:
- Guest name
- Reservation number
- Arrival and departure dates
- Room type and room number
- Status

From the popup you can navigate to the full reservation detail.

## Filters

| Filter | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Room Type | Filter the grid to show only rooms of a specific type. |
| Floor | Filter by floor number. |
| Status | Filter by room status (vacant, occupied, OOO, etc.). |

Use filters to focus on a specific section of the property.

## Room Status Indicators

The room plan reflects the current housekeeping status of each room. Rooms that are **Out of Order** or **Out of Service** are shown differently from vacant rooms, so you can avoid assigning them.

For detailed room status management, use the **Room Status** tab in [Housekeeping Management](/docs/operations/housekeeping/room-status).

## Common FAQs

**What is the difference between Housekeeping Room Plan and Front Desk Room Plan?**
They show the same grid. The Front Desk Room Plan is accessed from the Front Desk section of the sidebar; the Housekeeping Room Plan is accessed from the Housekeeping section. Use whichever is more convenient for your workflow.

**Why does a room show as occupied when the guest has checked out?**
The reservation may not have been checked out yet, or the room status has not been updated. Check the reservation status and update the room status in Housekeeping → Room Status.

**I clicked a vacant cell but the reservation form did not open.**
You may not have permission to create reservations. Ask your administrator to grant reservation creation permission.

**The room plan shows the wrong date range.**
Adjust the Start Date and End Date controls to the range you need.
