# Cashier Shift

**Cashier Shift** is used to open and close **cash register** shifts and view shift summary. Shift reports help reconcile daily cashier activity before **night audit**.

## Access

From the sidebar: **Cashiering** → **Cashier Shift**. You must complete **Cash Register Login** first if prompted.

## Opening a Shift

- **Open** a shift — Declare starting float and register.

## Closing a Shift

- **Close** a shift — Declare expected vs actual, and any variances.

Resolve variances per your property's policy.
